### Slide 2: Brief about the idea
**Annapurna: Logistics Perfected by AI**
Annapurna is a predictive AI logistics platform that eradicates food waste in transit. We monitor live truck telemetry to predict cargo spoilage *before* it happens. If a cold-chain failure occurs, the AI instantly pushes the distressed cargo to a geo-fenced Emergency Wholesaler Marketplace, allowing local buyers to bid on the food before it rots on the highway.

---

### Slide 3: Approach & Impact
*   **How did you approach the problem using Google Cloud technologies?**
    We used **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash** as our core Decision Engine. Instead of static rules, Gemini evaluates live truck telemetry (temperature, ethylene levels, ETA, traffic) in real-time. If it detects an anomaly (e.g., a compressor failure), Gemini calculates the exact spoilage window and autonomously decides whether to reroute the truck or trigger an emergency liquidation to nearby wholesalers.
*   **What real-world problem does your solution address?**
    It bridges the gap between predictive fleet maintenance and economic recovery. When a truck breaks down today, the food is thrown away. Annapurna recovers the fleet's financial loss and saves the food by instantly finding a buyer for it.
*   **What is the core architecture?**
    A Next.js frontend connected to a real-time Supabase PostgreSQL database. The data flow is governed by our Gemini-powered AI agent, which processes live truck telemetry and transforms it into actionable JSON payloads for live WebSockets dispatch to the Wholesaler Marketplace.

---

### Slide 4: Opportunities & USP
*   **How different is it from existing ideas?**
    Current telematics only tell you that a truck *has already broken down*. Annapurna proactively predicts failures and actually solves the economic fallout by selling the cargo while it's still good.
*   **USP of the proposed solution:**
    **The Emergency Wholesaler Marketplace.** By combining AI fleet tracking with a live B2B bidding platform, we turn a catastrophic logistical failure into a recovered asset with zero human intervention required.

### Slide 5: List of features offered
1.  **AI Fleet Command Center:** Powered by Google Gemini 1.5 to monitor ambient temperature, humidity, and truck health.
2.  **Emergency SOS & Liquidation:** Autonomous detection of cold-chain failures that pushes cargo to the live market.
3.  **Live B2B Bidding System:** Geo-located wholesalers receive instant alerts and can place partial orders or counter-bids on distressed cargo.
4.  **Real-Time Tracking:** Once a bid is accepted, wholesalers get turn-by-turn live GPS tracking to the truck's exact location.

### Slide 6: Process flow diagram

1.  **Anomaly Detection:** Gemini AI detects a temperature spike (e.g., > 18.5°C) and calculates the spoilage window.
